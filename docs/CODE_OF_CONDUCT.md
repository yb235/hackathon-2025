# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free hackathon experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

✅ **Be respectful** of different viewpoints and experiences
✅ **Be collaborative** and supportive of fellow participants
✅ **Be inclusive** and welcoming to all skill levels
✅ **Give and accept constructive feedback** gracefully
✅ **Focus on learning and building** together

## Unacceptable Behavior

❌ **Harassment** of any kind (verbal, physical, or visual)
❌ **Discriminatory** language or actions
❌ **Disruptive behavior** that interferes with others
❌ **Sharing others' private information** without permission
❌ **DoS attacks or system abuse** (especially Track C participants)

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

**During the event:**
- Approach any hackathon organizer (look for special badges)
- DM `@zekunwu_73994` on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/QBTtWP2SU6?referrer=luma)

**All reports are confidential** and will be handled promptly.

## Consequences

Violations may result in:
1. Warning from organizers
2. Removal from the event
3. Disqualification from prizes
4. Ban from future Holistic AI events

## Track C Ethical Guidelines

Participants in **Track C (Security & Red Teaming)** must:

✅ Test only provided endpoints
✅ Respect rate limits
✅ Use findings for education and defense

❌ **No DoS attacks** (immediate disqualification)
❌ **No attacking external systems** without permission
